<h1>Key Contributions</h1>
• Lead developer on volunteer team of 5 for pathfinding puzzle game, built with C# in Unity<br>
• Developed >100 levels, 11 puzzle mechanics, 4 cutscenes, ~5K words of dialogue<br>
• Built tile system, player input, bespoke pathfinding algorithm, undo history, save/load, VFX, UI/UX<br>
• Implemented shader effects including toon, vertex sway, depth and volumetric masks<br>
